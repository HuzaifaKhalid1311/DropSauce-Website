What is DropSauce?

DropSauce is an Android app for reading and organizing manga. It brings all the series you follow into one clean, customizable library, keeps track of what you've read, and stays out of your way — no ads, no sign-up to read, and no tracking. It's free and open source, built with a modern Material 3 Expressive design.

DropSauce does not host or provide any content of its own. Instead, you add the online sources you want to read from, and the app gives you a great reading and organizing experience on top of them.

Features

Personal library

Save your favourites and sort them into colour-coded categories that match how you actually read.

History & progress

Your reading history and exact per-chapter position are remembered, so you always continue where you left off.

Bookmarks

Bookmark individual pages and jump back to the moments you want to keep.

Tracking & stats

Follow the series you care about and see reading statistics over time.

Customizable reader

Choose the reading mode and layout that feel right, with a smooth, distraction-free experience.

Your sources

Add the online sources you want. The app ships with no built-in content — you stay in control of what you read.

Google Drive sync

Optionally sync your whole library between your devices using your own Google Drive. Off by default.

Private by design

No ads, no analytics, no account needed to read. Everything stays on your device unless you turn on sync.

Sync with your own Google Drive

DropSauce includes an optional sync feature so your library follows you across devices — stored in your own Google Drive, never on any server we run. Sync stays off until you choose to sign in.

1

Sign in

Sign in with your Google account from the app's sync settings. DropSauce only ever touches files it creates in your Drive.

2

Choose what syncs

Pick what to include — favourites, categories, history, progress, bookmarks, tracking, statistics and settings.

3

Stay in control

Sign out anytime, or delete all synced data from your Drive directly inside the app.

DropSauce can only access the files it creates in your Drive — it can't read or change any of your other files. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.
