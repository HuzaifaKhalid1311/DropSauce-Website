What is DropSauce?

DropSauce is an Android app for reading and organizing manga from online sources you add yourself. It lets you build a personal library, sort titles into categories, keep your reading history and progress, bookmark pages, and track series — all stored locally on your device.

Personal library

Save your favourites, organize them into colour-coded categories, and pick up exactly where you left off.

Reading history & progress

Your history and per-chapter reading position are kept so you never lose your place.

Bookmarks & tracking

Bookmark individual pages and track the series you follow, with reading statistics.

Open source

DropSauce is free and open source, distributed through GitHub. No ads, no accounts required to read.

Google Drive sync

DropSauce includes an optional feature that lets you sync your library between your own devices using your own Google Drive. Sync is off until you choose to sign in.

Your Drive, your data

Your library is stored as a single file inside your personal Google Drive. It never passes through any server we control.

You choose what syncs

Pick which parts to sync — favourites, categories, history, progress, bookmarks, tracking, statistics and settings.

You stay in control

Sign out at any time, or delete all synced data from your Drive directly inside the app.

When you enable sync, DropSauce asks for permission to access only the files it creates in your Google Drive. It does not read or modify any of your other Drive files. Read the full details in our Privacy Policy.
